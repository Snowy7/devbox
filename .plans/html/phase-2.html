Safety and Recovery

Activation

  • Second-device setup under 10 minutes P50.

  • Policy warnings are actionable.

Cost

  • Blob packing limits object operation cost.
  • Generated directories suppressed.
  • Storage per active user monitored.

Trust UX

  • Every sync decision is explainable.
