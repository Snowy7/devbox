KPI framework

Measure the moment it just works.

The north star is successful folder continuations: cross-device resume sessions where the folder is ready and the developer can act without blocking conflict or manual recovery.

Overview Market Roadmap KPI MD

Primary Metrics

Metric Meaning Target
Activation to second device New users who connect a second device and open a synced folder within 7 days. 40% beta, 60% guided
Time to folder ready Second-device sign-in to first selected folder ready. P50 under 10 min, P90 under 30 min
Snapshot capture latency Filesystem change to durable local snapshot. P50 under 5 sec, P95 under 30 sec
Restore success rate Restore attempts that complete without manual support. 95%+
